Mathemania 2023

Mathemania 2023 is a 3-stage treasure hunt consisting of Math and Logical puzzles, organized by the Stamatics Society, the Mathematics and Statistics society of the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. The event is designed for mathematics enthusiasts, offering a platform for participants to engage in challenging problem-solving related to mathematics and logic. The format may include stages or rounds with various mathematical and logical challenges.

The theme of the event will be Star Wars. Have fun solving interesting problems as you make way across the vast emptiness of the galaxy in the shoes of a bounty hunter!

  • Q1. Can participants with limited knowledge of higher Mathematics join Mathemania 2020?
    A1. Absolutely! No question in Mathemania 2020 requires expertise beyond high school level. While some exposure to higher Math might be beneficial, it's not a prerequisite.
  • Q2. Is Mathemania exclusive to IITK, UG students, or specific individuals?
    A2. Not at all! Mathemania welcomes everyone interested in Mathematics, logic, and puzzles, regardless of their affiliation with IITK, academic level, or location.
  • Q3. Is Mathemania a team event or can individuals participate?
    A3. Participants have the flexibility to join individually or form teams of 2 or 3 members to take on the challenges of Mathemania.
  • Q4. Is there any cost associated with registration for Mathemania?
    A4. No need to worry about registration fees! Mathemania is entirely free for all participants, making it accessible to everyone.
