Mathemania 2023

Mathemania 2023 is a 3-stage treasure hunt consisting of Math and Logical puzzles, organized by the Stamatics Society, the Mathematics and Statistics society of the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. The event is designed for mathematics enthusiasts, offering a platform for participants to engage in challenging problem-solving related to mathematics and logic. The format may include stages or rounds with various mathematical and logical challenges.

FAQs

Q. Is Mathemania a team event or can individuals participate?
A. Participants have the flexibility to join individually or form teams of upto 4 members.

Q. Is there any cost associated with registration for Mathemania?
A. Mathemania is entirely free for all participants.

Other Questions

Q. Can participants with limited knowledge of higher mathematics participate?
A. Absolutely! No question in Mathemania require expertise beyond the high school level. While some exposure to higher Math might be beneficial, it's not a prerequisite.

Q. Is Mathemania exclusive to IITK or UG students?
A. Not at all! Mathemania welcomes everyone interested in mathematics, logic, and puzzles, regardless of their affiliation with IITK, academic level, or location.
