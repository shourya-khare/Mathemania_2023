Raktarpan

Blood cannot be manufactured in factories; it can only come from generous donors. To cater to this demand, Raktarpan was launched as a project under NSS IIT Kanpur with an unparalleled objective of solving the problem of blood shortage in India.Raktarpan acts as a channel connecting voluntary blood donors with those who need blood. It is a youth-run organization and provides free help and specially works to target the poor and the needy. Since its inception, the organization has grown a lot in terms of working for this cause. Raktarpan has developed a 360 degree solution to the problem of blood shortage.

Vivekananda Samiti

Vivekananda Samiti channels Swami Vivekananda's teachings into practical activities, fostering positive energy and contributing to the IIT-K community and nation-building. Events encompass talks on Vedanta, ancient India, and permaculture, alongside self-belief exercises and social services like health camps. Weekly discussions, Vedanta and Sanskrit classes, and the distribution of Vivekananda literature engage the youth. This year, emphasis was on enhancing students' soft skills, leadership, and personality development for campus placements, reflecting the Samiti's commitment to holistic individual growth and societal impact.

Pragati

Pragati is dedicated to fostering rural, social innovations that aim at leveling the playing field in development. With a commitment to equality, the organization empowers marginalized communities through educational initiatives and holistic programs. By nurturing resilience and inclusivity, Pragati contributes to the creation of a more just and balanced society, ensuring that progress reaches every corner.

Collaboration with other communities and clubs

The Community Welfare Cell collaborates seamlessly with various clubs and societies at IIT Kanpur, fostering a united effort towards social impact and welfare. By joining forces with diverse organizations, the cell maximizes its reach and effectiveness, channeling collective energies to address community needs and contribute positively to society. This collaborative approach enhances the impact of initiatives, ensuring a holistic and well-coordinated effort towards the betterment of the community and beyond.
